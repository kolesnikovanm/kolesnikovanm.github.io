UNKNOWN

He grew up…

He successfully graduated from school and university.

He has good work…

He has a new family, a lot of friends and a girlfriend…

But he has a dream to find his first family…

A few years he was looking for a place, where he was lost, on Google Earth…

How could he find her birth home?

What happened to his brother and family?

Has his dream come true?

I would recommend reading this book, because I was really amazed by his feelings and emotions.
