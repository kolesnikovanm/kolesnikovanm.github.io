RETURN

It began in India where he returned after twenty years. The reason for his trip was looking for his birth mother and her family.
He was born in a poor district of a little Indian city.
He lived with his mother, two brothers and baby sister. His father left the family when he was very little. He was describing that their family was so poor that he often went hungry, or how he went around to people’s houses to beg for any leftover food. Hunger forced him to steal, beg some food. His mother worked hard, but she didn’t earn enough money to feed the whole family.
Their home was a tiny room in a run-down building with brick walls, a muddy floor and a little fireplace.
When he was four, he and his elder brother went to the railway station to earn some money.
This night became a nightmare for them because he was lost and his life changed.
